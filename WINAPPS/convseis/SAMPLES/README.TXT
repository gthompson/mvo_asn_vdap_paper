
                         CONVSEIS v6.0

     Conversion program package for seismological data on PCs.

                 by Lani Oncescu & Mike Rizescu

                         (April 2001)


   Content: SAMPLES

SAMPLES contains the following data files:

ALPHA.SAC, in alpha-SAC format, for testing:
    ASAC2EQ, ASAC2SGA, ASAC2SUD
ASCII.ASC, in a general ASCII format, for testing:
    ASC2V1
CSS.HDR and CSS.DAT, in CSS format, for testing:
    CSS2GSE, CSS2SGA, CSS2SUDS
GSETT2.GSE, in uncompressed GSE format, for testing:
    GSE2SG, GSE2SUDS, GSE2GSE and RENCHGSE
HYPO.EVL, HYPO.EVT and HYPO.PHA, for testing:
    HYPO2GSE
K2.001, K2.002, K2.003 and K2.HDR, for testing:
    K22SUDS
KINEMETR.V1, in VOL1 format, for testing:
    V12SGA
KINEMETR.V2, in VOL2 format, for testing:
    V22EQ and V22V2
PCEQ.EQ, in PCEQ format, for testing:
    EQ2ASC, EQ2GSE, EQ2SEGY, EQ2SEIS, EQ2SG, EQ2SUDS, FILLEQ, RFEQ, SORTEQ,
    SUMEQ
PCQUAKE.WVM, in PC-Quake format, for testing :
    WV2ASC, WV2EQ and WV2GSE
PCSEGY.SGY, in PC-SEGY format, for testing:
    SEGY2ASC and SGY2SGY
TIM.PAR is a parameter file for IASPEI Recsec program, for plotting PCSEGY.SGY
PCSUDS.DMX, in PC-SUDS format, for testing:
    SUD2ASAC, SUDS2EQ, SUDS2GSE, SUDS2SGA, EVL2SUDS, FILTSUDS, SUDSINST, SUDSRW,
    SUDSTOOL, SUDXTR
PITSAASC.BHZ, in PITSA ASCII format, for testing:
    ASC2ASAC, ASC2SUDS
SEISGRAM.ASC, in SeisGram-ASCII format, for testing:
    SGA2ASC, SGA2EQ and SGA2SGA
SEISGRAM.BIN, in SeisGram-binary format, for testing:
    SG2EQ, SG2SEIS, SG2SUDS and SG2SG
820516.EQ is an output of SDP2EQ conversion.
